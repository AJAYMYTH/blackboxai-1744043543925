Frequently Asked Questions

What types of e-waste do you accept?

We accept all types of electronic waste including computers, laptops, mobile phones, tablets, TVs, printers, batteries, and other electronic devices.

How are rewards calculated?

You earn ₹5 for every kilogram of e-waste recycled. The weight is measured when we collect your items and the rewards are credited to your account within 24 hours.

Is there a minimum quantity for pickup?

No, we accept any quantity of e-waste. However, rewards are only applicable for pickups of 1kg or more.

How do I track my pickup request?

You can track your pickup status in real-time through your dashboard. We'll also send SMS and email notifications at each stage.
