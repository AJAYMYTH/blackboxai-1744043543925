Our Recycling Process

We use state-of-the-art industrial processes to safely recycle your e-waste and recover valuable materials.

1

Collection & Sorting

Your e-waste is collected from your location and transported to our facility where it's sorted by type (computers, phones, batteries, etc.).

2

Dismantling & Shredding

Items are manually dismantled to remove hazardous components, then shredded into small pieces for material separation.

3

Material Separation

Advanced separation techniques including magnetic, eddy current, and optical sorting recover metals, plastics, and glass.

4

Refinement

Recovered materials undergo further refinement to achieve purity levels suitable for manufacturing new products.

5

New Product Manufacturing

The recycled materials are sold to manufacturers who use them to create new electronics and other products.

Meet Our Machines

E-Waste Shredder

Powerful industrial shredders that break down electronics into small, uniform pieces for efficient separation.

Magnetic Separator

Extracts ferrous metals like iron and steel from the shredded material using powerful magnets.

Eddy Current Separator

Uses rotating magnetic fields to separate non-ferrous metals like aluminum and copper from other materials.

PCB Recycling Unit

Specialized equipment to safely process printed circuit boards and recover precious metals.

Dust Extraction System

Advanced filtration systems capture and contain any dust or particulate matter generated during processing.

Plastic Granulator

Processes recovered plastics into uniform pellets ready for reuse in manufacturing.

Environmental Benefits

Reduces Mining

Recycling 1 million laptops saves energy equivalent to 3,500 homes' annual electricity use.

Prevents Pollution

Proper recycling prevents heavy metals from contaminating soil and water sources.

Saves Energy

Recycling aluminum uses 95% less energy than producing it from raw materials.
